Step-By-Step

Making an Online Purchase

Step 1) Open your favorite web browser. For this example, I will use Google Chrome.

Step 2) In the Google search bar at the top of the screen, type Amazon.com, then press enter on your keyboard.

Step 3) Once the Amazon page has loaded, search aquarium in the search bar. Click the orange magnifying glass.

Step 4) Select the aquarium that you'd like to purchase and click the blue text under the picture of the product. This will open up the product details.

Step 5) On the right side of the screen, click the yellow Add to Cart button. This product will then be ready to purchase.

Step 6) Once the product is added to the cart, click the shopping cart icon on the upper right hand side of the screen. This will take you to your cart, which shows all of the products you wish to purchase.

Step 7) On the right side of the screen, select the orange Proceed to Checkout button.

Step 8) You'll be brought to a screen asking if you want to sign into your Amazon account or use a guest account. For this tutorial, we are signing into our own account.

Step 9) Enter your email and password and click Sign-In.

Step 10) Enter your shipping information and payment information, then click Place Your Order on the right side of the screen.

Step 11) You will be brought to a page to review your order, make sure everything is correct. If everything looks okay, you have completed your order. Your purchase will ship out shortly!
